- When freezing the compositor (SIGSTOP) before NVIDIA GPU suspend, apply the SIGSTOP as `ExecStartPre` on `nvidia-suspend.service` rather than via `powerManagement.powerDownCommands` (`sleep-actions.service`), because `nvidia-sleep.sh` does `chvt 63` inside `ExecStart` and races ahead of parallel `sleep-actions` — the SIGSTOP must be guaranteed to fire before the VT switch. Keep the SIGCONT + display re-probe as `ExecStartPost` on `nvidia-resume.service`. Confidence: 0.78
- When disabling USB/Thunderbolt wake sources on a laptop, avoid blanket `xhci_hcd` wake disable rules — they prevent wake from any USB device (including keyboards). Confidence: 0.65
